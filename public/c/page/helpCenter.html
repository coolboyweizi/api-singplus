    What if my vocal and the beats are not in sync?

    You can adjust your Vocal Timing in Preview page by moving forward or backward to play the vocal earlier or later.

    I can’t download the karaoke tracks, what can I do?

    Please make sure your phone is well connected to Internet, then close Sing+ app and restart again.
  • 3

    Why can’t I hear my voice after recording?

    First of all, please make sure you have allowed the recording permission for sing plus on your phone. If not, please enable it in your phone Settings. Secondly, please try to use earphones with a microphone while recording. By the way, the best distance between lips and microphone is 3~10 cm. Thirdly, sing out loud with the microphone. At last, please turn up the vocal volume and turn down the karaoke volume before saving.
  • 4

    What should I do if the app crashes?

    Please make sure to update Sing+ to the latest version. And try to restart Sing+ to see if the problem still exist.
